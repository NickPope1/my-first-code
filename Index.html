🍦 How to Prepare Ice Cream for Kids 🍦

Welcome! Here are some fun and easy ways to make ice cream that kids will love.

Easy No-Churn Ice Cream Recipe

What You'll Need:

  • 1 can of sweetened condensed milk
  • 2 cups of heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • Your favorite toppings (sprinkles, chocolate chips, fruit)

Steps:

  1. Pour the heavy cream into a bowl
  2. Whip it until stiff peaks form (about 5 minutes with a mixer)
  3. Gently fold in the sweetened condensed milk and vanilla extract
  4. Pour into a container and add your favorite mix-ins
  5. Freeze for at least 6 hours
  6. Scoop and enjoy!

Fun Flavor Ideas for Kids

  • Strawberry (add pureed fresh strawberries)
  • Chocolate (add cocoa powder)
  • Mint Chocolate Chip (add mint extract and chocolate chips)
  • Birthday Cake (add sprinkles and cake pieces)

Tips for Success

  • Let kids help with mixing - it's fun!
  • Keep all ingredients cold before starting
  • Don't skip the whipping step - it creates the creamy texture
  • Store in an airtight container for best results

Have fun making delicious ice cream with your kids!
